Diffrence between var, let, const

var: Scope: Function-scoped. Variables declared with var are function-scoped, meaning they are only accessible within the function they are declared in.
let: Scope: Block-scoped. Variables declared with let are block-scoped, meaning they are only accessible within the block (enclosed by curly braces) they are declared in.
const: Scope: Block-scoped. Similar to let, variables declared with const are block-scoped.
